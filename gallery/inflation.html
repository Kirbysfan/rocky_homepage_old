My belly... Is inflating like a balloon!

    One day I came out finding a package of meow chocolate cookie in my mailbox. It didn't say where did it come from.
    However, I still accepted, took it to the backyard and started enjoying.
    I didn't feel something weird when I took the first bite, but a few moments later, I started... Feeling abnormal...
    I discovered that my belly is growing bigger, just like a balloon being inflatted!
    Sooner, my belly became round and big, just like a balloon. I can hardly move... Ah... I'll never eat things indiscriminantly again!
